Features * An extensible service provider framework that greatly simplifies the process of connecting local user accounts to hosted provider accounts. * A connect controller that handles the authorization flow between your Java/Spring web application, a service provider, and your users. * Java bindings to popular service provider APIs such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, TripIt, and GitHub. * A sign-in controller that enables users to authenticate with your application by signing in through a service provider. * Much more... ##
